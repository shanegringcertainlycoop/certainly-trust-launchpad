---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Content velocity drops | Batch-write guides; use Dispatch pipeline as proof of consistency |
| Low domain authority slows rankings | Focus on low-competition keywords first; build links via guest posts |
| Competitor starts content marketing | First-mover advantage + weekly Dispatch cadence is hard to match |
| Service pages don't convert | Add clear CTAs, partnership inquiry forms, testimonials to each |
| Keyword cannibalization | Maintain keyword mapping doc; one primary keyword per page |
