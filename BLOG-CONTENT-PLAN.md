## Content Brief Template

Each post should follow this structure for maximum SEO and AI citation value:

1. **Opening definition/answer** (first 100 words) — answer the primary query directly
2. **Context and background** — why this matters for certification bodies specifically
3. **Step-by-step or structured sections** — use H2/H3 hierarchy, numbered lists, tables where appropriate
4. **Real examples** — reference Certainly's client work (IWBI, Delos, CodeGreen, etc.)
5. **FAQ section** (3-5 questions) — boosts AI citation readiness and long-tail keyword coverage
6. **CTA** — link to relevant service page or partnership dialog
